# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users

FAANG hiring managers, senior engineering leaders, and technical recruiters. They arrive with a specific job to do: evaluate whether Giacomo Sortino is a credible candidate for a Director/EM role with AI transformation credentials. They're scanning quickly — the interface must communicate authority and differentiation within seconds, not minutes.

### Brand Personality

**Three words:** Innovative. Authoritative. Forward-thinking.

Emotional goal: The visitor should feel "this person is ahead of the curve." Not flashy — sharp. The interface should signal someone who thinks in systems, ships with precision, and leads from the front on AI adoption. Confidence without arrogance.

### Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme:** Dark mode only — the existing violet/slate direction is correct and should be refined, not replaced
- **Primary:** Violet as brand anchor. Slate neutrals. Accent colors (sky, emerald, orange) used purposefully for data visualization
- **Anti-references:** Avoid generic corporate/recruiter-targeted aesthetics. No light-mode LinkedIn vibes. No "AI slop" tells (gradient text, glassmorphism as decoration, glow borders, cyan-on-dark)
- **Typography:** Currently Inter — fine for body, but could be elevated with a more distinctive display font for headings
- **Motion:** Scroll-triggered reveals and counter animations are appropriate for this context; keep purposeful, not decorative

### Design Principles

1. **Scan-first hierarchy** — Hiring managers don't read, they scan. Every section must communicate its core value in 3 seconds before rewarding deeper reading.
2. **Data over decoration** — Every visual element should serve communication. Metrics, impact, and outcomes are the content; decoration must earn its place.
3. **Confident restraint** — Dark + violet is the right call. Resist adding more effects; instead refine spacing, contrast, and typographic hierarchy.
4. **Motion with purpose** — Animations reveal information (counter reveals, content expansion) rather than performing for performance's sake.
5. **WCAG AA minimum** — Text contrast, keyboard navigation, and semantic HTML are non-negotiable. This is a professional context.
